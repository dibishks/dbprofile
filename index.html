Agriculture

Agriculture & My Dream Farm

Nature has always been close to my heart, and farming is something I truly love. My dream is to start a farm in Idukki, where we have some land nestled in the forest, surrounded by mountains. The idea of growing fresh produce in such a beautiful setting excites me.

These days, I spend most of my free time farming—growing vegetables and flowering plants. It gives me a sense of peace and fulfillment. Looking ahead, I want to turn this passion into something bigger. My goal is to start a small business in agriculture, setting up a store in my village to sell my own farm products. Eventually, I hope to establish my own brand and share my love for natural, homegrown food with more people.

Farming is not just a hobby for me; it’s a vision for the future! 🌱🌾
